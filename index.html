Meet The Founder

B. Meenakshi

Founder & Lead Bridal Artist

From a young age, B. Meenakshi was captivated by the artistry of beauty — the way a thoughtful look could transform not just a face, but the confidence of the woman wearing it. That early passion quietly grew into a calling.

After her marriage, she turned her lifelong love for makeup into Saghas Bridal Makeup Aesthetic, a studio devoted entirely to bridal beauty. Every bride is treated as an individual — her features, her culture, her story — with looks crafted to feel authentically her.

Dedicated to a deeply personalized experience, premium products and uncompromising hygiene, Meenakshi brings calm, professionalism and genuine warmth to every appointment — so you can simply relax and feel beautiful on your most important day.

  • ✦ Passion for beauty from a young age
  • ✦ Studio founded after marriage, devoted to bridal artistry
  • ✦ Personalized, one-to-one client experience
  • ✦ Professional service with a commitment to hygiene & care
